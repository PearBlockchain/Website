Our Story for Web3.0

We provide insurance for web3 assets, designed specifically for creators and digital native businesses.

We believe creators and digital-native SMB are underserved when it comes to protection and insurance. Digital businesses suffer from risks covered by cyber insurance - but cyber insurance is too expensive and sometimes covers a wide variety of risks, not necessarily needed by small business owners.

We believe the solution is to create a cyber-insurance policy, tailor-made for creators and SMB, which protects a specific digital asset (e.g social media accounts, eCommerce stores, NFT and gaming assets).
